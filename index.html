About

Katie Masley is a trained birth doula, experienced childbirth educator, and maternal health consultant based in Pittsburgh, PA. With over six years of experience coordinating a community-based doula program, Katie brings a powerful blend of compassion, strategy, and lived expertise to her work.

Through Bridge & Bloom, she partners with organizations to build equitable, community-centered maternal health initiatives. Whether she's training doulas, designing public health programs, or facilitating transformative conversations about birth equity, Katie’s work is rooted in the belief that every birthing person deserves support, dignity, and joy.
